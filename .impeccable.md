## Design Context

### Users
- Primary users are homeowners pairing a single smart plug during first-time setup.
- They use the app in everyday home contexts, often on mobile, and want setup completed quickly with minimal technical friction.
- Core job-to-be-done: successfully pair one plug to Wi-Fi and cloud in one guided session.

### Brand Personality
- Friendly, warm, and reassuring.
- Should feel trustworthy and calm during failure-prone pairing steps.
- Language should prioritize plain guidance over technical terminology.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Keep visual style approachable and practical for home users.
- Prioritize clarity and progress confidence over decorative complexity.
- Use supportive guidance patterns (clear progress, explicit next actions, reassuring feedback).

### Design Principles
1. One clear primary action per step, especially during pairing-critical moments.
2. Reduce cognitive load by minimizing choices early and revealing alternatives progressively.
3. Preserve state and continuity across wizard and provisioning handoff.
4. Prefer plain-language microcopy over protocol/transport jargon.
5. End with a confident success moment that reinforces completion and control.
